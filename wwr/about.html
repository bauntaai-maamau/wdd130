History

Since its establishment five years ago, Bak04 Rafting Company has grown from a small, passionate team of river enthusiasts into a leading rafting company known for its thrilling expeditions and exceptional service. Founded in 2019 by avid adventurers Bootika and Taoaba Bakoa, the company began with just a single raft and a vision to share the exhilaration of white-water rafting with others. Over the years, Rapids Rush has expanded its fleet and developed a variety of routes catering to all experience levels, from tranquil family floats to adrenaline-pumping rapids. Their commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and unforgettable customer experiences has earned them a loyal following and numerous accolades within the outdoor adventure community.
